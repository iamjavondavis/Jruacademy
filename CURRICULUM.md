# Jru Academy Reading Curriculum

## Objective

Build Jru's comprehension from strong second-grade foundations toward confident third-grade work without relying on random or endless content.

## Mastery standard

- 4 questions per story
- 75% required to master a story
- A missed skill should be repeated in a new context before moving forward
- Stories increase gradually in length, vocabulary, and reasoning difficulty

## Level 1 — Literal understanding

1. Main idea and details
2. Sequence of events
3. Who, what, where, when
4. Basic vocabulary in context
5. Problem and solution

## Level 2 — Connecting ideas

6. Cause and effect
7. Character traits
8. Compare and contrast
9. Beginning, middle, and end
10. Using story details as evidence

## Level 3 — Deeper comprehension

11. Inference
12. Prediction
13. Author's message or lesson
14. Multiple-meaning words
15. Summarizing in one or two sentences

## Level 4 — Advanced second-grade bridge

16. Point of view
17. Fact versus opinion
18. Text structure
19. Explaining an answer with evidence
20. Comparing two short passages

- Longer passages
- Stronger vocabulary

### Batch 3: Stories 21–30

- Third-grade bridge passages
- Paired texts
- Evidence-based answers
- Short written responses
- Weekly boss-battle review

## Controlled AI content rules

Future AI-generated lessons should:

1. Target one clearly named reading skill.
2. Stay within the assigned reading level.
3. Use age-appropriate themes and language.
4. Include one correct answer and plausible distractors.
5. Be reviewed by a parent before publishing.
6. Avoid replacing the planned curriculum sequence.
7. Track which skill the story is designed to measure.

## Recommended data fields for every lesson

- Level

- Story
- Questions
- Answer choices

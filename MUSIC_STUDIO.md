# Jru Academy Music Studio

## Purpose

Music Studio teaches Jru to listen closely, name the parts of music, and build original songs in GarageBand. Artist links are examples for listening and ear training. Lessons do not instruct students to copy a copyrighted recording.

## Current artist library

- Michael Jackson — dance beat, bass, guitar, claps, and layered vocals
- Travis Scott — deep bass, roomy drums, repeating patterns, and atmosphere
- Bruno Mars — pop, funk, horns, guitar, bass, and melody
- Stevie Wonder — keyboards, chords, bass, drums, and voice
- Pharrell Williams — drum patterns, claps, bass, and musical space
- Earth, Wind & Fire — horns, bass guitar, drums, rhythm guitar, and group vocals
- Daft Punk — electronic drums, synthesizers, loops, and robot-style sounds
- Hans Zimmer — strings, brass, drums, piano, and movie-music dynamics

Apple Music buttons open artist search pages. A parent chooses the exact song and clean version.

## Eight-lesson path

1. Hear the Parts of a Song
2. Build Your First Drum Beat
3. Add a Strong Bass Line
4. Make Chords and a Melody
5. Make a Dance Groove
6. Make a Space Beat
7. Mix the Song
8. Share Your Original Song

## GarageBand teaching standard

Every build lesson should include:

- The exact place to tap first
- Suggested tempo range
- Instruments or track types to choose
- A small first pattern before adding complexity
- Clear track length or bar count
- Volume and hearing-safety reminders
- A project checklist
- A clear file name or save step
- Parent approval before sharing

Directions should use language a third- or fourth-grader can follow. Explain new words such as tempo, BPM, bass, chord, melody, pan, echo, and reverb when they first appear.

## Originality rule

Students may study a broad musical idea such as a dance groove, deep bass, funk rhythm, or space mood. They should not copy a song's melody, lyrics, recording, artwork, or exact arrangement.

## App support

Jru Academy includes a web app manifest, app icon, service worker, and Home Screen directions. The core Academy can cache for faster return visits. Apple Music, GarageBand links, videos, and 3D features may still require an internet connection.
